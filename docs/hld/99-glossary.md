# 99 - Glossary

## A-Z Glossary

### C&D Waste

Construction and Demolition Waste

### Gatepass Fee

Entry fee charged for vehicle access to waste management facility

### LPR (License Plate Recognition)

Automated optical character recognition technology for vehicle identification

### MEG/SEN/SIT Zones

- **MEG**: Main Entry Gate
- **SEN**: Inbound Scale (Entry to Site)
- **SIT**: Outbound Scale (Exit from Site)

### Telnet/M-Bus/Modbus

Industrial communication protocols for device integration

### Trip Lifecycle States

- **INITIATED**: Trip started at main gate
- **WEIGHED_IN**: Inbound weight captured
- **WEIGHED_OUT**: Outbound weight captured
- **COMPLETED**: Trip finalized and billing calculated

### UHF RFID

Ultra High Frequency Radio-Frequency Identification

### ZKBio CVSecurity

ZKTeco's access control and vehicle management platform
